Welcome to my website!

My name is Gabriel Ferreira Silva Tibúrcio and here you will find both the papers we have published and the slides we have used for presentations. I finished my PhD in theoretical computer science at University of Brasília, Brazil. My advisor was Mauricio Ayala-Rincón and my co-advisor was Maribel Fernández. We worked with Daniele Nantes and Temur Kutsia. I recently started working as a data analyst for the Brazilian government at TJDFT and I am not doing much academic work nowadays.
